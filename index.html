YALE SCHOOL OF ART

The Yale School of Art is a graduate school that confers MFAs in graphic design, painting, printmaking, photography, and sculpture, and offers undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students.

Read More

  • Graphic Design

    Graphic design is a creative field that involves the art and practice of visual communication.

  • Painting

    Painting encompasses a rich tapestry of techniques, styles, and historical significance.

  • Printmaking

    Printmaking is an artistic process that involves creating artworks from one surface to another.

  • Sculpture

    Sculpture is the art of shaping materials like clay or wood into visual pieces.

Furniture Study Highlights Tour

The Leslie P. and George H. Hume American Furniture Study Center at Yale's West Campus is offering a one-hour in-person tour, showcasing over 1,300 pieces of American furniture and clocks from the 17th century to present day, along with a contemporary wood art collection.
